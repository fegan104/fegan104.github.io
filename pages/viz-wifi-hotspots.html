WiFi Hotspots

Created by the Venice Project Center

The WiFi Hotspots web application is designed to allow users to see all of the locations providing VeniceConnected WiFi, a public WiFi service offered at many locations in the historical center as well as other islands, and the Venetian mainland. This information is displayed as data points on a map, with each point of WiFi service radiating a rough estimation of its range as a small circle on the map.

Click on the floating button in the bottom right-hand corner to check it out!

Visualizing the continuum of data in Venice
